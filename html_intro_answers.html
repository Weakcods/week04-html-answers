Marvel Universe: Heroes & Anti-Heroes

Explore the rich tapestry of Marvel's cinematic universe, from Earth's mightiest heroes to complex anti-heroes, each bringing their unique stories to life on the big screen.

Venom

A fascinating anti-hero story following journalist Eddie Brock as he becomes the host of an alien symbiote, granting him incredible powers while forcing him to share his body with a hungry alien entity.

Key Characters

Eddie Brock - A journalist turned symbiote host

Venom - An alien symbiote seeking redemption

The Avengers

Earth's mightiest heroes must come together to combat threats no single superhero could face alone. The team's dynamics and personal conflicts add depth to their epic battles.

Key Characters

Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, Hawkeye

Iron Man

The story of Tony Stark, a genius billionaire who transforms from a weapons manufacturer to a high-tech superhero, protecting the world in his advanced armor.

Key Characters

Tony Stark/Iron Man - Genius, billionaire, philanthropist

Pepper Potts - His loyal partner and CEO

Hulk

The story of Bruce Banner, a scientist working for the U.S. military, became the Hulk after being exposed to gamma rays. He's on the run, pursued by the government for military hardware. He seeks a cure and faces terror of the Hulk's appearance..

Key Characters

Bruce Banner/The Hulk - scientist

Black Widow

Black Widow is a former KGB agent Natasha Romanova, better known as Black Widow, is one of the best agents S.H.I.E.L.D. has ever had. She's a longtime member of the Avengers as well as the leader of the short-lived Los Angeles-based superhero team: The Champions.

Key Characters

Natasha Romanova/Black Widow - Former KGB agent
